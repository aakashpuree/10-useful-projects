# 🚀 AI Crash Course Prompt Generator

Welcome to the **AI Crash Course Prompt Generator**! 🎓✨ 

This sleek, Apple-inspired web application is designed to help students and lifelong learners instantly generate structured, high-efficiency study prompts for any AI model (like ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini). 

Stop wrestling with prompt engineering and start studying smarter!

## ✨ Features

- **🍎 Premium Apple-Style UI**: A clean, minimalistic, and highly responsive design that looks stunning on any device.
- **⚡ Supercharged Inputs**: Easily enter your exam details, including Subject, Available Time, Chapters, and specific Topics.
- **🤖 Model Preferences**: Tailor the output by selecting your favorite AI model for optimized prompt structuring.
- **👁️ Real-Time Preview**: See your prompt being generated live as you type.
- **📋 One-Click Copy**: Instantly copy your perfect prompt to your clipboard and paste it into your AI of choice!

## 🛠️ Built With

- **HTML5**: Semantic structure for a solid foundation.
- **CSS3 (Vanilla)**: Custom, beautiful styling with glassmorphism, smooth animations, and zero heavy frameworks.
- **JavaScript (ES6)**: Real-time logic, dynamic previewing, and clipboard functionality.

## 🚀 Getting Started

No installation required! Just open it in your browser and start generating:

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open `index.html` in your favorite modern web browser.
3. Fill in your study requirements.
4. Click generate, copy your prompt, and paste it into your AI assistant!

## 💡 Why This Project?

We believe that learning should be accessible and efficient. By automating the prompt engineering process, you can spend less time figuring out *how* to ask an AI for help, and more time actually learning the material.
